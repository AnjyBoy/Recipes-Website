Taco Skillet

A Mouth-Watering Recipe for delicious taco skillet with all fixings

This easy, one-pan taco skillet recipe is a perfect weeknight meal. It's made with ground beef,beans,cheese, and other taco toppings, and cooked in a skillet

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 cup converted rice
  • 1 (1 ounce) package taco seasoning
  • 1 cup shredded Mexican cheese blend
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce

Steps

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium heat
  2. Cook and stir beef and onion until beef is browned, about 5 minutes
  3. Drain. Stir tomatoes, water, rice, and taco seasoning into beef mixture and bring to a boil.
  4. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer until rice is tender, about 25 minutes.
  5. Top with Mexican cheese blend and lettuce before serving.
